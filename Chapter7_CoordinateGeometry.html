Chapter 7: Coordinate Geometry

Distance, Section Formula, Midpoint, and Slope

Important Formulas

1. Distance Formula:

d = √[(x₂-x₁)² + (y₂-y₁)²]

2. Midpoint Formula:

M = ((x₁+x₂)/2, (y₁+y₂)/2)

3. Section Formula (m:n ratio):

P = ((mx₂+nx₁)/(m+n), (my₂+ny₁)/(m+n))

4. Slope:

m = (y₂-y₁)/(x₂-x₁)

5. Area of triangle with vertices (x₁,y₁), (x₂,y₂), (x₃,y₃):

A = 1/2 |x₁(y₂-y₃) + x₂(y₃-y₁) + x₃(y₁-y₂)|

Examples

Distance Formula

Find distance between A(3,4) and B(0,0):

d = √[(3-0)² + (4-0)²] = √[9 + 16] = √25 = 5

Midpoint Formula

Find midpoint of P(4,6) and Q(8,10):

M = ((4+8)/2, (6+10)/2) = (6, 8)

Slope

Find slope between (2,3) and (5,9):

m = (9-3)/(5-2) = 6/3 = 2
