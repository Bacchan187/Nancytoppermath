Chapter 11: Areas Related to Circles & Volumes

Sectors, Segments, and 3D Shapes

Area Formulas

1. Area of Sector: A = (θ/360°) × πr²

2. Arc Length: l = (θ/360°) × 2πr

3. Area of Segment: Area of Sector - Area of Triangle

Volume Formulas

1. Cube: V = a³

2. Cuboid: V = l × b × h

3. Cylinder: V = πr²h, SA = 2πr(h + r)

4. Cone: V = 1/3 πr²h, SA = πr(l + r)

5. Sphere: V = 4/3 πr³, SA = 4πr²

6. Hemisphere: V = 2/3 πr³, SA = 3πr²

Examples

Sector Area

Find area of sector with θ=90°, r=4cm:

A = (90/360) × π(4)² = 1/4 × 16π = 4π cm²

Cylinder Volume

Find volume of cylinder with r=3cm, h=5cm:

V = π(3)²(5) = 45π cm³
